Para generar el .o se usa:
g++ -IC:C:\SFML-2.6.0\include -I "C:\SFML-2.6.0\include" -c hello.cpp -o hello.o

Para generar el .exe se usa:
g++ -LC:\SFML-2.6.0\lib .\hello.o -o hello.exe -lmingw32 -lsfml-graphics -lsfml-window -lsfml-system -lsfml-main -mwindows
